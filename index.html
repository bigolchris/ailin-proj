Introduction

This Portfolio was made for a personal interest project for an English class. The concept or essential question that this project revolves around was, “How can the influence of different photographers with different backgrounds add more meaning behind my photographs?”. To answer this question I decided that I was going to make a portfolio of photographs after researching a couple of photographers and their work. The key elements that I was trying to focus on was, what made the photographs meaningful and what gave the photographs soul. The photographers that I centered my attention on were, Jesus Manuel Garza, Oscar B. Castillo, Henri Cartier-Bresson, Ernest Cole and Graciela Interbide.
